C: \About

Starting out as a social media coordinator at my first full-time job after college, it was only myself and one other person in charge of our small company's digital department. I quickly needed to take on a bigger role in order to help with the influx of digital requests from our clients. At this job, I was introduced to the world of website maintenance and have since taken it upon myself to research front-end web development on my own time with the focus of creating a career out of it. I love coding because I love learning and building things. I enjoy challenging myself every day and working through problems.
